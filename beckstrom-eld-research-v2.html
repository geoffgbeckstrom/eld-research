Your research roadmap
As you read your sources, look for answers to these questions:
  • Who — who is being hurt? who is causing it?
  • What — what is actually happening?
  • Why — why is it happening? who benefits?
  • When — when did it start?
  • Where — where exactly is it happening?
  • How — how does it hurt people?
